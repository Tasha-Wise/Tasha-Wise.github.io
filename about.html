Tasha Wise imagines the world beyond an average human to understand mythical creatures and explore their lives. Exploring the world of magic and how a world with an added element can differ from everyday life. Using hidden details to help piece together this world as bigger whole past the panel. Guided by fears, nightmares, and dreams to bring digital pieces to life on the screen. Looking past the supernatural veil to show a bigger world out past the shadows.

She has an A.A Digital Arts degree at Hartnell Community College and is currently working on a B.A. Studio Practice degree and San Jose State University. The Journey of transferring from a community college to state college giving her a different view on college life. Her obsession with mythical creatures have fuel her desire to draw and create them in a form for all to enjoy easily.
